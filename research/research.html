Core Research

Segmentation for all granularity

I build segmentation networks which are invariant to instance imbalance. Including Semantic/Instance/Panoptic and Part Aware models. In collaboration with BraTS, I build highly optimized metrics and evaluation pipelines to support this as well. Do remember, metrics are painstakingly slow in 3D, hence optimization of them is a key aspect of my work.

Kernels for Segmentation

I work on building triton kernels specifically to enhance segmentation pipelines.

Federated Learning on the Edge

With Yang Li, I work on hardware and software to deploy FL pipelines. I also work on building hyper-optimized neural networks to function on the edge.

Parallelism for Large Models

Continuing part-time with Cosine, I explore and implement parallelism strategies to accelerate large model training and inference, including data, tensor, and pipeline parallelism. My work focuses on optimizing distributed computation for efficiency and scalability.

Data Acquisition for Large Code Models

I design and implement scalable pipelines for acquiring, filtering, and curating high-quality code datasets to train large code models. My work addresses challenges in data diversity, deduplication, and quality assurance to improve model performance and reliability.
